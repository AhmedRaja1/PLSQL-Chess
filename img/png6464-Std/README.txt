64X64 orance-style chess-set is the original graphics from PigBase4 (1995).

A design with a carefully selected color-scheme to give a relaxing, ergonomic experience.
At the same time all pieces have mouths, to support graphic presentation of individual 'happiness'.
Castling-rights and en-passant is visually supported too.
